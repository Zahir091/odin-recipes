Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 28 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 1 tbs italian seasoning
  • 15 oz whole milk ricotta
  • 3.5 cups shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • Quarter cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbs chopped parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles in a pot with salted water
  2. Heat olive oil in large pan
  3. Add ground beef and onions. Cook until beef is brown
  4. Add tomato sauce and italian seasoning
  5. Preheat Oven to 350F
